What is Doulingo English Test

The Duolingo English Test is an online exam that candidates can take on their personal computers. Candidates need to prove English language proficiency can give the Duolingo English Test which can be taken online from anywhere anytime. Like conventional study abroad exams TOEFL, IELTS and PTE, it also tests all the four language skills Reading, Writing, Listening, and Speaking. In this post, we will inform you of everything about the Duolingo test and whether you should take it or not. The test is adaptive which means the difficulty level of the test increases with every correct answer and the next question becomes easier if your answer is wrong. Although the test duration is only around an hour but only with few questions it can test the ability of the test taker quickly. Candidates can take the test from their home and no appointment needed for taking the test, as candidates can take it anytime anywhere as per their convenience. It also has an upgraded section where candidates can take a video interview of 10 minutes and record their answers to open-ended questions.

Why Duolingo English Test?

In this time of COVID-19 crisis when students are not able to give the English Language Proficiency exams in the test center, Duolingo offers them the opportunity to attempt the test from their homes. Although not all the colleges are accepting the Duolingo test scores. Currently, the Duolingo exam is majorly used for admission to undergraduate programs in the US. IELTS and TOEFL exams are accepted by thousands of programs. Also, with Duolingo, one can send scores to as many educational institutions as one wants for free, whereas, with IELTS and TOEFL exams one has to pay a $20 fee for sending score reports to any educational institution other than what they are allowed at the time of registration.

How can I take the Duolingo test?

You can take the test online anywhere, anytime. Candidates need not travel to a test center for attempting this test. Candidates can take the test from their home as per their convenience they just need some necessary equipment to give it. As candidates will be taking the test from their home, Duolingo makes sure that candidates follow the stringent rules it had set in place, so, you take the test without any kind of help. Candidates need to be alone in the room while taking the test and nobody should be talking to you at the time of the test. Also, aspirants can't navigate from the browser at the time of taking the test. Apart from this, there are other set of rules which you need to follow while taking the exam.

Difference between Duolingo English Test and TOEFL Exam

The items of the Duolingo test are often brief in comparison to other English Language Proficiency Exams. Whereas, the TOEFL exam focuses on scenarios you are going to witness in the real world particularly in academics. Also, questions in the TOEFL exam are divided into different sections of Reading, Writing, Listening, and Speaking whereas the Duolingo test includes all types of questions in one exam. This also means you have to adapt quickly to solve the next question. If one question tests your Reading skills then the other one may test your Listening skills. Also, the Duolingo test is adaptive which means the difficulty level of questions is related to the previous question, whereas, in the TOEFL exam, the difficulty level does not have anything with your previous answers.

Difference between Duolingo English Test and IELTS Exam

Dulolingo test can be given in one attempt whereas the IELTS exam is given generally over a two- day period. Also, there are various differences in the format of both the exams. Although both the exams test all the four skills of the English language, however, you will not see long texts in the Duolingo exam. IELTS exam tests the candidate through essay questions, short answer, and multiple-choice questions while the Duolingo test the candidate's ability on the use of intonation and knowledge of idioms.

What happens after the test

Once you complete the test, it is sent to the proctors and the candidate receives an email stating when he will get the scores (48 hours). When the scores are ready, the candidate is notified through an email. Candidates can take two certified tests within a 30-day period. A certified test is a usual test that you give and get scores. If, in case you face any technical issue during the exam, it will not be count against the two-time limit. Duolingo scores are valid for two years. Once you receive your scores you can send it to the educational institutions straight from the results page and there is no limit of sending the score reports. You can send the score report to as many institutions you want.
